App Privacy Delete Data Support

Last updated: July 7, 2026 · Innovator Generation

No account required. Passport Photo Grid does not store your photos or personal data on our servers. There is no cloud account to delete.

What data exists?

  • Photos: Kept in app memory only while you use the app.
  • Preferences: Country, paper size, grid, and background settings saved locally on your device.
  • Server data: None. We do not operate user accounts or photo storage.

Delete data in the app

  1. Open Passport Photo Grid.
  2. On the Photo tab, tap the ✕ button to clear your uploaded photo.
  3. Go to the app menu → Privacy Policy → tap Clear Local App Data to reset all saved preferences.

Delete data on iOS

  1. Follow the in-app steps above.
  2. To remove the app completely: long-press the app icon → Remove App → Delete App. This removes all local app data.

Delete data on Android

  1. Follow the in-app steps above.
  2. Or go to Settings → Apps → Passport Photo Grid → Storage → Clear data.
  3. To remove everything: Uninstall the app.

Delete data on Web

  1. Open the app and clear your photo in the Photo tab.
  2. Clear saved preferences via Privacy Policy → Clear Local App Data.
  3. Clear this site’s browser storage:
    • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → select this site’s data.
    • Safari: Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data → remove passport-photo.

Need help?

If you have questions about data deletion, visit our Support page or read the full Privacy Policy.
