Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app upload my photos?
No. All processing runs on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers.

Which paper sizes are supported?
4×6 (landscape & portrait), 5×7, 8×10, and A4.

Background replacement not working?
Works best with a solid, plain background behind the subject. Busy backgrounds may not remove cleanly.

Where can I print?
Any photo lab or store that prints your selected paper size. Use cutting guides to trim each photo.

How do I delete my data?
See our Delete Data page.
